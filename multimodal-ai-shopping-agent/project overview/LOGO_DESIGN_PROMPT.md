# 🎨 Shopping AI Assistant Logo Design Prompt

This document contains a professional image generation prompt for the **Shopping AI Assistant** project, designed following the **Ultimate Prompting Guide for Nano Banana** (Google Cloud) principles.

## 🚀 The Prompt

Copy and paste the following prompt into **Imagen 3**, **Gemini**, or your preferred AI image generator:

> **Persona:** Senior Brand Identity Designer specializing in high-tech retail and AI startups.
>
> **Context:** "Shopping AI Assistant" is a state-of-the-art multimodal AI shopping assistant. It enables users to search for products using both images and text simultaneously. The tool is known for its extreme speed (<300ms) and high precision. The target audience is modern, tech-savvy consumers and retailers.
>
> **Task:** Design a minimalist, premium, and iconic logo for "Shopping AI Assistant".
>
> **Visual Requirements:**
>
> - **Iconography:** A stylized, geometric symbol that evokes "multimodal search" and "instant product discovery". Consider a minimalist camera lens or eye integrated with a search magnifying glass or a fluid "S" shape that looks like a scanning laser or a neural network node.
> - **Aesthetic:** Clean, professional, and modern. Flat design with subtle 3D depth (glassmorphism accents). Use smooth, high-quality gradients.
> - **Typography:** "Shopping AI Assistant" in a clean, sans-serif, modern typeface (like Inter, Outfit, or a custom geometric font). The text should be positioned below or to the right of the icon.
> - **Color Palette:** Professional and vibrant. Primary: Electric Indigo or Deep Space Blue. Accent: Neon Cyan or Vibrant Orange.
> - **Background:** Solid white or dark grey to emphasize the premium feel.
>
> **Format:** High-resolution vector style, center-aligned, 8k, photorealistic rendering for the glassmorphism effects, masterwork quality.
>
> **Negative Prompt:** Cluttered, busy design, cartoonish, low quality, blurry, shadows, complex patterns, overlapping text, multiple versions in one image.

---

## 💡 Prompting Strategy (Based on Google Guide)

The prompt above leverages the **Persona-Context-Task-Format** framework highlighted in the Google Cloud guide:

1. **Persona:** Setting the role ensures the AI adopts the right professional tone and design standard.
2. **Context:** Providing the "why" (multimodal AI, speed) helps the AI translate abstract speed and intelligence into visual elements.
3. **Task:** Clear instruction on what to create (minimalist, premium logo).
4. **Negative Constraints:** Ensures the output avoids common "AI-looking" mistakes like clutter or messy text.

## 🛠️ Customization Tips

- **For a Dark Mode Version:** Change "Background: Solid white" to "Background: Deep charcoal black with neon glow accents".
- **For a More "Retail" Feel:** Add "Include a subtle geometric shopping bag silhouette integrated into the icon".
- **For App Icon focus:** Specify "Designed to fit within a rounded square app icon frame, centered on a gradient background".
